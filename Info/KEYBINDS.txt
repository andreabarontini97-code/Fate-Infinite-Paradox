KEYBINDS

Mouse:
	Pointer: Free Movement
	Mouse1: Select
	Double Click Mouse1: Confirm Skill Use/Movement
	Mouse2: Open Status Window
	Mouse2 (With Open Status Window): Open Noble Phantasm (Final Blow) Details 

Keyboard:

	WASD: Tile-Specific Movement
	Z: Buster (Offensive) Skill
	X: Arts (Defensive) Skill
	C: Quick (Movement) Skill
	V: Noble Phantasm (Final Blow)
	Enter: Confirm Skill Use/Movement
	Spacebar: Open/Close Windows
	Ctrl: Simplified Birdeye View Of Whole Map
